Registration

How to register with the Comparative Pathology Workbench

From the User Menu on the Right-Hand Side of the Main Menu, click on the Registration Menu Option; Your registration is confirmed by email.

How to register with the Comparative Pathology WordPress Server

You will also need to register on the Comparative Pathology WordPress site to ensure you can use the full functionality of the Workbench.


Authorisation

Once registered with the Comparative Pathology Workbench and WordPress servers, your accounts need to be confirmed by the Comparative Pathology Workbench Administrators.
